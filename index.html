Greg's 40th Birthday

Chose your own adventure...

Four decades of fire, of family, of fight,
Greg’s journey has shimmered, a bold, brilliant light.
He’s climbed every mountain, blazed every road,
Built a fine life with a meaningful load.

Now comes a moment to breathe, to rest—
Before tackling what’s next (and giving your best).
You’ve earned a break, a rejuvenating quest,
To reset your soul and feel truly blessed.

So Greg, the choice is now up to you:
Beachside Grande Dunes with an ocean view?
Or Chetolah’s peaks in Blowing Rock air?
Both will be hot, but peace waits there.
